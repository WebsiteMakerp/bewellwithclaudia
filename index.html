About Claudia

Claudia is a dedicated wellness coach with over 10 years of experience empowering people to live healthier, happier lives. She believes true wellness is a balance of mind, body, and spirit — tailored uniquely to each individual.

Her approach combines evidence-based techniques with compassionate support to guide you through lifestyle changes, stress management, nutrition, and mindfulness practices that stick.

Claudia’s mission is to help you unlock your full potential and create sustainable habits for long-lasting vitality and joy.

  • Mindfulness & Meditation Training – learn techniques to reduce stress and increase mental clarity.
  • 1-on-1 Support Sessions – ongoing motivation and accountability to keep you on track.
